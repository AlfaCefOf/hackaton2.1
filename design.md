# Pet Tashkent UI/UX Concept

## Overall Concept & Visual Identity

**Style:** Modern minimalism combined with soft illustrative elements to create a friendly, approachable feel.

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Light turquoise (#A8DADC)
- Secondary: Mint green (#F1FAEE)
- Accent: Soft orange (#F4A261)
- Background: Creamy white (#FEFEFE)
- Text: Dark gray (#2F3E46) for readability

**Visual Mood:** Warm, community-driven, trustworthy, and safe for pets. Use rounded corners (8-12px radius), soft drop-shadows (blur 4-8px, opacity 0.1-0.2), glassmorphism for sections (backdrop blur, semi-transparent backgrounds), and subtle gradients.

**Typography:**
- Primary Font: Inter (clean, modern sans-serif)
- Headings: Inter Bold/SemiBold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Sizes: H1: 48px, H2: 36px, H3: 24px, Body: 16px, Small: 14px

**Elements:** Floating paw shapes, subtle 3D icons, high-definition pet illustrations or curated real photos.

## Main Dashboard Layout

### Top Navigation Bar
- **Logo:** "Pet Tashkent" with a cute paw icon (stylized SVG: rounded paw print in light turquoise).
- **Menu Items:** Home, Buy Pets, Adoption, Foster, Donations, Vet Map (horizontal list, hover underline animation).
- **Right Side:** Login button (soft orange background, rounded), user avatar (circular, with notification badge), notifications icon (bell with red dot for unread).
- **Background:** Semi-transparent white with blur effect for glassmorphism.
- **Height:** 64px, fixed at top.

### Hero Section / Main Banner
- **Layout:** Full-width section, height 70vh, centered content.
- **Visuals:** Large illustrated pets (dogs, cats, parrots) arranged in a collage style, with floating paw shapes and subtle 3D icons.
- **Text:** Headline "Connecting Tashkent through Pets â€” Buy, Adopt, Donate & Care" in H1, subtext in body font.
- **CTA Buttons:** "Browse Pets" (primary turquoise button), "Find Nearby Vets" (secondary mint green outline).
- **Background:** Soft gradient from creamy white to light turquoise, with overlay illustrations.

### Search & Filters Section
- **Search Bar:** Wide input field (rounded, 48px height), placeholder "Search by breed, type, city district", with search icon.
- **Category Chips:** Horizontal scrollable chips for Dogs, Cats, Birds, Reptiles, Other Pets (selected state: turquoise background).
- **Filter Dropdowns:** Price (range slider), Age, Gender, Vaccinated, Shelter/Private Owner (dropdown menus with checkmarks).
- **Layout:** Below hero, full-width, white background with subtle shadow.

### Pet Marketplace Section
- **Grid Layout:** Responsive grid (3-4 columns on desktop, 1-2 on mobile), 16px gap.
- **Pet Cards:**
  - Image: 250x200px, rounded corners.
  - Info: Age, breed, location (small text).
  - Tags: Badges like "For Sale", "Adoption", "Temporary Foster", "Free Giveaway" (colored chips).
  - Highlight Badges: "Vaccinated", "Healthy", "Good with kids" (green checkmarks).
  - Buttons: "Buy" (orange), "Contact Owner" (turquoise), "Adopt Now" (mint).
- **Interactions:** Hover lift (translateY -4px), micro-animations on buttons.

### Adoption & Foster Area
- **Layout:** Dedicated panel below marketplace, full-width.
- **Content:** Emotional photos, storytelling cards with pet backgrounds (e.g., "Meet Luna, rescued from the streets").
- **CTAs:** "Meet this Pet", "Request Temporary Care", "Adopt for Free" (buttons with heart icons).
- **Color Accents:** Soft orange for emotional highlights.

### Donation / Charity Section
- **Layout:** Clean grid of shelter cards.
- **Shelter Cards:** Photo, location, animal count, urgent needs, bank details.
- **CTA:** "Donate to Shelter" (large button).
- **Visuals:** Hearts, paws, progress bars for fundraising goals (animated fill).

### Interactive Tashkent Vet Clinic Map
- **Map UI:** Polished Google Maps style, soft blue theme.
- **Pins:** Veterinary clinic icons (paw + cross).
- **Info Cards:** On click, overlay card with name, address, rating (stars), services, call button.
- **User Location:** GPS marker.
- **Layout:** Full-width section.

### Footer
- **Links:** About, Privacy, Contact, Animal Welfare Rules.
- **Social Media:** Icons for Facebook, Instagram, etc.
- **Design:** Minimalist with tiny paw patterns.

## Logo Suggestions
1. **Primary Logo:** Text "Pet Tashkent" in Inter Bold, paw icon in turquoise.
2. **Icon Variations:** Rounded paw print, heart-shaped paw, minimalist line art.

## Mobile-Responsive Variations
- Navigation: Collapsible hamburger menu.
- Hero: Stacked layout, smaller images.
- Grid: Single column on mobile.
- Map: Touch-optimized pins.

## Micro-Animations and Interactions
- Button hover: Scale 1.05, color shift.
- Card hover: Lift and shadow increase.
- Map pin: Bounce on load.
- Search: Typing animation, filter expand.
